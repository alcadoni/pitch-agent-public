## Long-Term Vision

Pitch Agent is being developed as an autonomous multi-agent reasoning platform capable of replacing fragmented startup advisory processes with a single AI system that combines strategic reasoning, market analysis, narrative development and validation into coherent founder guidance and investor-ready communication.
